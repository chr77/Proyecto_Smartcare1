Departments

  • Technological Development Department
  • Response and Assistance Department
  • Monitoring and Support Department
  • Communication and Marketing Department
  • Research and Development Department

Technological Development Department

Responsible for designing and developing the emergency collar. Responsible for the integration of technologies such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and Arduino into the device. Work on product sustainability using biodegradable materials.

Response and Assistance Department

Responsible for implementing the emergency response system. Develops the notification mechanism to emergency services in case of lack of response from the user within the established period. It operates the support center that communicates with users through the collar's built-in speaker.

Monitoring and Support Department

Manages the camera system for remote monitoring of users. Offers technical support to users and family members who use the application and the emergency collar.

Communication and Marketing Department

Responsible for communicating the benefits of the SmartCare system to the public. Develop marketing strategies to reach the target audience. Create advertising content that highlights the innovation, security and peace of mind provided by SmartCare.

Research and Development Department

Researches and develops continuous improvements to the SmartCare system. It is at the forefront of emerging technologies that could be integrated into future versions of the product.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover more about SmartCare and clear your doubts to make the right decision. Here you will find answers to the most common questions that will help you understand how our comprehensive assistance and alert system is designed to improve the safety and quality of life of disabled people and older adults.

  • How does the SmartCare emergency collar work?

    The collar has an emergency button that, when pressed, activates an alert in the application. This process initiates a sequence of responses to ensure immediate assistance in emergency situations.

  • What security measures have been implemented to protect user privacy?

    SmartCare prioritizes privacy. The data transmitted is encrypted, and only designated family members and caregivers have access to the information through the app. We are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality of our users.

  • How are emergency services notified in the event of a failure to respond to the alert?

    If there is no response to the alert within 20 seconds, our system automatically notifies emergency services. This feature ensures an additional safety net for vulnerable people.

  • Is the monitoring app compatible with different phone models and smart devices?

    Yes, the application is compatible with IOS and Android operating systems. Plus, it integrates with popular smart devices like Alexa and Google Assistant for a complete and versatile experience.

  • How is the durability of the emergency collar guaranteed in daily and environmental conditions?

    The collar is designed with waterproof and biodegradable materials, such as cork. In addition, the internal components, such as the Arduino and lithium battery, are selected to withstand daily use and various environmental conditions.
